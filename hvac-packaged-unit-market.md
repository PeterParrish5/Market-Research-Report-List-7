<p><p>The HVAC packaged unit market is primarily categorized into air-cooled and water-cooled condensers. Air-cooled condenser units utilize ambient air to dissipate heat, making them ideal for smaller applications or regions with limited water resources. In contrast, water-cooled condensers rely on chilled water systems for heat exchange, offering higher efficiency and better cooling performance, suitable for larger facilities. Both types cater to diverse commercial and industrial needs, with energy efficiency and installation preferences driving market demand.</p></p>
