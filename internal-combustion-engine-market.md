<p><p>The internal combustion engine market is primarily classified into two types: 2-stroke and 4-stroke engines. 2-stroke engines complete a power cycle in two strokes of the piston, allowing for a simpler design and lighter weight, making them ideal for small applications like chainsaws and lawn mowers. In contrast, 4-stroke engines require four strokes to complete a cycle, featuring better fuel efficiency and lower emissions, making them prevalent in automobiles and larger machinery. Each type serves distinct market needs based on performance and application.</p></p>
