<p><p>The orthodontic archwires market comprises various types, primarily including beta titanium, nickel titanium, and stainless steel. Beta titanium offers excellent elasticity and strength, making it suitable for complex cases. Nickel titanium, known for its superelastic properties, provides gentle forces for tooth movement and is often used in early treatment phases. Stainless steel, characterized by its durability and rigidity, is commonly used for finishing stages due to its ability to maintain shape under stress. Each type serves distinct clinical needs in orthodontics.</p></p>
