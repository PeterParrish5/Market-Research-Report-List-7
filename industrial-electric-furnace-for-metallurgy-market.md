Electric Arc Furnaces utilize high-voltage electricity to generate heat for melting scrap steel and other metals, making them efficient and environmentally friendly. Induction Furnaces use electromagnetic induction to melt metals, known for precise temperature control and energy efficiency. The "Others" category includes various specialized furnaces, such as resistance and lifting furnaces, catering to specific metallurgical processes and applications.</p></p>
