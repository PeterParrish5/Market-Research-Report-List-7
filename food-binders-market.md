<p><p>The food binders market can be categorized into four main types: Sugar Type, Starch Type, Protein Type, and Gel Type. Sugar Type binders are derived from sweeteners and provide texture and cohesion. Starch Type binders use various starch sources to thicken and stabilize food products. Protein Type binders utilize proteins for emulsification and moisture retention, enhancing texture. Gel Type binders, such as gelatin or pectin, create a gel-like consistency, improving mouthfeel and structure in a variety of food applications.</p></p>
