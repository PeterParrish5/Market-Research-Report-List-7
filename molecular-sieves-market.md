Type 4A has a larger pore size of 4 angstroms, targeting molecules like ethanol and smaller hydrocarbons. Type 13X can adsorb larger molecules, enhancing applications in petrochemical and natural gas processing. Other market types include customized sieves tailored for specific industries, further expanding their utility in various separation processes.</p></p>
