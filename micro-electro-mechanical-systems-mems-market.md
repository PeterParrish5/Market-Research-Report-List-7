<p><p>Micro-Electro Mechanical Systems (MEMS) are tiny devices integrating mechanical and electrical components at a microscale, widely used in various applications. The MEMS market can be categorized into fixed and movable types. Fixed MEMS devices, like accelerometers, have stationary components that sense changes in motion. In contrast, movable MEMS devices, such as micro mirrors used in projectors, feature components that can move or actuate, allowing for dynamic functionality. Both types play crucial roles in automotive, medical, and consumer electronics industries.</p></p>
