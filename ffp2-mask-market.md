<p><p>The FFP2 mask market can be categorized into two primary types: valveless masks and breather valve masks. Valveless masks provide a tighter seal and are designed to filter inhaled and exhaled air effectively, ensuring maximum protection in various environments. Breather valve masks, on the other hand, feature one-way valves that facilitate easier exhalation, reducing heat and moisture buildup inside the mask. Both types serve to protect against airborne particles, but their functionality and comfort levels can differ significantly based on usage scenarios.</p></p>
