<p><ul><li>Thermosetting</li><li>Thermoplastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CFRP (Carbon Fiber Reinforced Polymer) market consists of two primary types: thermosetting and thermoplastic. Thermosetting CFRP is created through a curing process that hardens the material irreversibly, offering high strength, rigidity, and excellent thermal resistance. It's commonly used in aerospace and automotive applications. In contrast, thermoplastic CFRP is characterized by its ability to be reshaped upon heating, allowing for quicker manufacturing and recycling possibilities. This type is gaining traction in industries that prioritize flexibility and efficiency.</p></p>
