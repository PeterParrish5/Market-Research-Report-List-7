<p><p>The automotive exhaust system gaskets market comprises three primary types: metal gaskets, non-metal gaskets, and composite gaskets. Metal gaskets, often made from steel or aluminum, offer high durability and heat resistance, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Non-metal gaskets, typically crafted from materials like rubber or silicone, provide excellent sealing capabilities while being cost-effective. Composite gaskets combine both metal and non-metal materials, delivering enhanced strength and flexibility, ideal for varied exhaust system demands, ensuring optimal performance and longevity.</p></p>
