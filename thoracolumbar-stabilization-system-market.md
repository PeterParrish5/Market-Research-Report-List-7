<p><p>The Thoracolumbar Stabilization System market is primarily categorized into two types: Posterior Stabilization System and Anterolateral Stabilization System. The Posterior Stabilization System focuses on providing support through the back muscles and vertebrae, often utilizing rods, screws, and plates to enhance spinal stability. In contrast, the Anterolateral Stabilization System emphasizes a more integrated approach, addressing spinal support from the front and sides, which can be crucial for certain spinal conditions. Both systems aim to improve patient outcomes in spinal surgeries.</p></p>
