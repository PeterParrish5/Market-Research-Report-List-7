<p><p>The Maritime Satellite Communication market comprises two key types: Very Small Aperture Terminal (VSAT) and Mobile Satellite Services (MSS). VSAT systems involve fixed satellite communications that provide high-speed internet and data services on vessels, enabling efficient operations and connectivity. In contrast, MSS offers mobile connectivity through satellite networks, catering to ships in remote areas or at sea. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing maritime communication, supporting navigation, safety, and operational efficiencies in the shipping industry.</p></p>
