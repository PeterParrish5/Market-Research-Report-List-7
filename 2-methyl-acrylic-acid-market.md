<p><p>The 2-Methyl-Acrylic Acid market is primarily categorized by production methods: the Acetone Cyanohydrin Method and the Isobutylene Oxidation Method. In contrast, the Isobutylene Oxidation Method utilizes isobutylene, oxidizing it to generate the acid. Each method influences production economics, environmental impact, and application suitability, leading to distinct market dynamics and opportunities for manufacturers and consumers engaged in various industries.</p></p>
