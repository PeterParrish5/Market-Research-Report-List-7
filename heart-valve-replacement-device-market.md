<p><p>The heart valve replacement device market encompasses several key types: </p><p>Mechanical heart valves, made from durable materials, provide long-lasting support but may require lifelong anticoagulation therapy. Tissue heart valves, derived from animal or human tissue, offer a more natural feel and typically don't require long-term anticoagulants, but have a shorter lifespan. Transcatheter heart valves are minimally invasive devices delivered via catheter, ideal for high-risk patients. Each type caters to diverse patient needs based on health status, age, and lifestyle considerations.</p></p>
