<p><ul><li>Cryogenic Distillation</li><li>Pressure Swing Adsorption</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid nitrogen market primarily comprises two types of production methods: cryogenic distillation and pressure swing adsorption (PSA). Cryogenic distillation involves cooling gases to extremely low temperatures to separate nitrogen from air, ensuring high purity levels suitable for industrial applications. In contrast, pressure swing adsorption utilizes adsorbent materials under varying pressure conditions to selectively separate nitrogen from other gases. Both methods serve diverse industries, including healthcare, food processing, and metal production, driving demand for liquid nitrogen in various applications.</p></p>
