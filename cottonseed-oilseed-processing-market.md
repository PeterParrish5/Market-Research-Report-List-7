<p><p>Cottonseed oilseed processing involves extracting oil from cottonseeds through mechanical or chemical methods. Mechanical processing uses physical means like pressing to crush seeds and extract oil, retaining natural properties and nutrients, suitable for small-scale operations. Chemical processing employs solvents like hexane to dissolve and separate oil, yielding higher efficiency and larger volumes, ideal for industrial-scale production. Both methods cater to different market needs, with mechanical processing focusing on purity and chemical processing emphasizing cost-effectiveness and scalability in the edible oil and industrial sectors.</p></p>
