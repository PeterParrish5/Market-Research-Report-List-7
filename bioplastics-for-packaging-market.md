<p><p>The bioplastics packaging market encompasses various types, each derived from renewable resources. PLA (Polylactic Acid) is a biodegradable choice from corn starch, while PHA (Polyhydroxyalkanoates) is produced by microbial fermentation. Starch blends combine natural starch with other materials for versatility. Other types include niche bioplastics catering to specific needs.</p></p>
