<p><strong>The Mammography Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Computed Radiography Detectors</li><li>Flat Panel Detectors</li><li>CMOS Flat Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mammography detectors are essential in breast cancer screening, with three main types: Computed Radiography (CR) Detectors, which use imaging plates to capture data and require a scanning process; Flat Panel Detectors, which convert X-rays directly into digital signals, offering high image quality and quicker results; and CMOS Flat Detectors, utilizing complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology for enhanced resolution and lower radiation doses. Each type provides unique advantages in terms of image clarity, efficiency, and patient safety during mammographic examinations.</p></p>
