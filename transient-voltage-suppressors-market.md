<p><p>The transient voltage suppressors (TVS) market is divided into uni-polar and bi-polar types, each serving specific applications. Uni-polar TVS devices are designed to protect against voltage spikes in a single direction, making them suitable for DC and low-frequency AC circuits. Bi-polar TVS devices, on the other hand, can handle voltage transients in both directions, making them ideal for AC applications and sensitive electronic circuits. The choice between these types depends on the protection needs and application requirements in various industries.</p></p>
