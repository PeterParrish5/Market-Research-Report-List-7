<p><p>The Fluorescent Microsphere market includes two primary types: Hollow Microspheres and Solid Microspheres. Hollow Microspheres are lightweight, with a hollow core, offering advantages like low density and high surface area, making them ideal for applications requiring buoyancy or insulation. Solid Microspheres, on the other hand, are dense and uniform, providing stability and consistent fluorescence, suitable for precise labeling and imaging in biomedical research. Both types are widely used in diagnostics, drug delivery, and flow cytometry, catering to diverse industrial and scientific needs.</p></p>
