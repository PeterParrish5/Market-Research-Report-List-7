<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Mark Pen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aqueous Mark Pen</li><li>Oily Mark Pen</li><li>Alcohol Mark Pen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mark pens are categorized into three main types based on their ink formulation: aqueous, oily, and alcohol-based. Aqueous mark pens use water-based ink, making them non-toxic and suitable for paper surfaces, but prone to smudging. Oily mark pens contain oil-based ink, offering vibrant colors and durability on various surfaces, including plastics; however, they can take longer to dry. Alcohol mark pens utilize alcohol-based ink, providing quick-drying capabilities and intense pigmentation, ideal for artists and detailed projects, particularly on non-porous surfaces.</p></p>
