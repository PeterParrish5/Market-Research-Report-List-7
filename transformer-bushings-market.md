<p><p>The Transformer Bushings market encompasses three main types: Solid Type, Resin Impregnated Paper, and Oil Impregnated Paper. Solid Type bushings offer durability and are typically used in environments with minimal electrical stress. Resin Impregnated Paper bushings provide excellent electrical insulation and thermal stability, suitable for high-voltage applications. Oil Impregnated Paper bushings, commonly used in transformers, offer high dielectric strength and cooling properties, making them ideal for high-performance energy transfer. Each type serves distinct applications in electrical distribution and power generation.</p></p>
