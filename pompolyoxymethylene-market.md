The POM market is typically segmented into two main types: POM-H (homopolymer) and POM-C (copolymer). POM-H offers greater strength and stiffness, making it ideal for precision parts, while POM-C features improved chemical resistance and processing flexibility, making it suitable for diverse applications, including electronic components and consumer goods.</p></p>
