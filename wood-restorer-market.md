<p><p>The wood restorer market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and oil-based wood restorers. In contrast, oil-based wood restorers penetrate deeper, providing longer-lasting protection and enhanced durability, suitable for exterior applications. Each type caters to specific needs, such as ease of use or resilience, allowing consumers to choose based on their project requirements.</p></p>
