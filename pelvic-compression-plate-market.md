<p><p>The Pelvic Compression Plate market is segmented into locked and unlocked types based on their design and application in orthopedic surgery. Locked plates feature screws that secure bone fragments more rigidly, providing enhanced stability and promoting faster healing, ideal for complex fractures. Unlocked plates offer greater flexibility and ease of installation, allowing for dynamic stabilization. Both types cater to different surgical needs, with locked plates typically used in more challenging cases, while unlocked plates are suited for simpler fractures.</p></p>
