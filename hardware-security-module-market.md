<p><ul><li>LAN Based</li><li>PCle Based</li><li>USB Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hardware Security Module (HSM) market is categorized into three main types: LAN-based, PCIe-based, and USB-based modules. LAN-based HSMs connect through a local area network, allowing multiple clients to access the secure cryptographic services remotely. PCIe-based HSMs are installed directly on servers via the PCIe interface, providing high-speed performance for intensive workloads. USB-based HSMs offer portability and convenient access, connecting to devices via USB ports, making them ideal for individual users or small businesses seeking secure cryptographic solutions.</p></p>
