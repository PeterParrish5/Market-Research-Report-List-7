The market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Pure Antigen**: These kits include unlabelled antigens used to generate specific antibodies for assay development. </p><p>2. **Radio Labelled Antigen**: These kits use antigens tagged with radioisotopes, enabling direct measurement of antigen-antibody interactions. </p><p>3. **Antibody Market**: This segment focuses on kits containing radioactively labelled antibodies, essential for detecting specific antigens in samples. Each type serves distinct roles in immunological research and diagnostics.</p></p>
