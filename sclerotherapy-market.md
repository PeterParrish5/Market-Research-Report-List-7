<p><p>Sclerotherapy involves the injection of solutions to treat varicose veins and spider veins. The market for sclerotherapy is categorized into three main types: detergents, osmotic agents, and chemical irritants. Detergents enhance the cleansing action on vein walls, leading to their closure. Osmotic agents create osmotic pressure that damages endothelial cells, promoting vein collapse. Chemical irritants cause localized inflammation, which helps in sealing off the affected veins. Each type serves a unique mechanism for effective sclerotherapy treatment.</p></p>
