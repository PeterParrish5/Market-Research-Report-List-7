<p><p>The car air bed market is primarily segmented into two types based on material: PVC and TPE. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) air beds are popular for their durability and affordability, offering good waterproofing and easy maintenance. In contrast, TPE (thermoplastic elastomer) air beds are known for their flexibility, lightweight nature, and eco-friendliness, providing a more comfortable sleeping surface. Each type caters to different consumer preferences, with PVC being favored for practicality, while TPE appeals to those seeking innovation and sustainability.</p></p>
