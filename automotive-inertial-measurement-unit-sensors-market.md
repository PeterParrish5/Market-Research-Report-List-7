<p><ul><li>MEMS gyroscope-based IMUs</li><li>FOG-based IMUs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive inertial measurement unit (IMU) sensors market primarily comprises MEMS gyroscope-based IMUs and FOG-based IMUs. MEMS gyroscope-based IMUs utilize micro-electromechanical systems to provide precise motion sensing, offering advantages like low cost and compact size, making them suitable for various automotive applications. Conversely, FOG-based IMUs use fiber optic technology for high accuracy and reliability in challenging environments, essential for advanced safety systems and autonomous vehicles. Each type serves distinct needs in the evolving automotive landscape.</p></p>
