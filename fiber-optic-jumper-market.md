<p><ul><li>Single-mode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optic jumper market features two main types: single-mode and multimode fibers. Single-mode fibers have a smaller core diameter, which allows only one mode of light to propagate, offering high bandwidth over long distances, making them ideal for telecommunications and data centers. In contrast, multimode fibers have a larger core, allowing multiple light modes, which is suitable for shorter distances, such as within buildings or campuses. Each type serves specific networking needs based on distance, capacity, and application requirements.</p></p>
