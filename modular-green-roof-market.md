<p><p>The modular green roof market encompasses three main types: intensive, semi-intensive, and extensive. Intensive green roofs feature deep soil systems supporting a wide variety of plants, including trees, offering recreational spaces. Semi-intensive roofs provide moderate soil depth, allowing for diverse plant life, while accommodating some landscaping options. Extensive green roofs have shallow soil and are designed for low-maintenance, drought-resistant vegetation. Each type caters to different needs, from aesthetic appeal to environmental benefits, influencing their adoption and market growth.</p></p>
