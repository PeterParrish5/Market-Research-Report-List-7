<p><p>The Gadolinium-based Contrast Media market is categorized into three main types: Extracellular Agents, Blood Pool Agents, and Hepatobiliary Agents. Extracellular Agents are used for general imaging, enhancing visibility in various tissues. Blood Pool Agents remain within the vascular system for prolonged imaging, aiding in cardiovascular assessments. Hepatobiliary Agents target the liver and biliary system, providing specific information for liver-related conditions. Each type addresses distinct diagnostic needs, contributing to the overall growth of the contrast media market.</p></p>
