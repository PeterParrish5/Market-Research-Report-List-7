<p><p>In the Fluid Catalytic Cracking market, two prominent types are the side-by-side type and the stacked type. Conversely, the stacked type has reactors arranged vertically, optimizing space and enhancing efficiency for larger refineries. Both configurations aim to maximize the conversion of heavy hydrocarbons into valuable lighter products, catering to varying refinery capacities and operational needs.</p></p>
