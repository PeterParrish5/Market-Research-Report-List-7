<p><p>The coal tar market is categorized into three types based on temperature during production. High Temperature Coal Tar, produced at over 1000°C, is primarily used in the production of carbon products and roofing materials. Medium Temperature Coal Tar, generated at 500-1000°C, is utilized for producing pavements and sealing agents. Low Temperature Coal Tar, obtained below 500°C, serves applications in various industries, including pharmaceuticals and paints. Each type offers distinct properties and applications tailored to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
