<p><p>Wireless ambulatory telemetry monitors are categorized into three main types: telemetry monitors, receiving equipment, and other equipment. Telemetry monitors are wearable devices that continuously track and transmit patients' vital signs like heart rate and rhythm. Receiving equipment includes stationary or mobile receivers that process and display the transmitted data for real-time monitoring by healthcare professionals. Other market components may consist of accessories, software, and integration systems that enhance the functionality and usability of these monitoring solutions in clinical settings.</p></p>
