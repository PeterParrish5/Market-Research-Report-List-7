<p><p>The bio stimulants market is categorized into humic-based, amino-based, and fulvic-based products. Fulvic-based products feature smaller molecular structures that enhance nutrient absorption and retention in plants and soils. Each type plays a pivotal role in boosting agricultural productivity and sustainability, addressing the increasing demand for environmentally friendly farming solutions.</p></p>
