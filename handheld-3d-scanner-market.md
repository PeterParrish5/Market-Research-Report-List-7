<p><p>The handheld 3D scanner market is primarily divided into two types: structured light scanners and laser scanners. Structured light scanners project a series of light patterns onto an object, capturing its shape and dimensions through image analysis, making them ideal for detailed scans of smaller objects. In contrast, laser scanners use laser beams to measure surface distances, offering high precision over larger areas and complicated geometries. Both types cater to diverse applications in industries like manufacturing, healthcare, and heritage preservation.</p></p>
