<p><ul><li>Embolization Coils</li><li>Flow Diversion Stents</li><li>Liquid Embolic Systems</li><li>Aneurysm Clips</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The neurovascular embolization device market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Embolization Coils** are used to occlude blood vessels, particularly in aneurysms.</p><p>2. **Flow Diversion Stents** redirect blood flow away from the aneurysm, promoting healing.</p><p>3. **Liquid Embolic Systems** involve injectable materials that solidify upon contact with blood, blocking blood flow effectively.</p><p>4. **Aneurysm Clips** are surgical devices used to permanently close off an aneurysm's neck, preventing blood flow into it. Each type offers distinct advantages for treating various neurovascular conditions.</p></p>
