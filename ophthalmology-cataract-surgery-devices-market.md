<p><p>The ophthalmology cataract surgery devices market encompasses various essential tools used in cataract procedures. Intraocular lenses (IOLs) are implanted to replace the clouded natural lens, enhancing vision. Phacoemulsification devices utilize ultrasound technology to break down cataracts, facilitating their removal. Ophthalmic viscoelastic devices (OVDs) maintain eye shape, protect intraocular structures, and aid in IOL implantation. Together, these devices enhance surgical outcomes, improve patient safety, and contribute to the overall effectiveness of cataract surgery procedures.</p></p>
