<p><p>The Ink Wetting Agents market can be categorized into two primary types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based ink wetting agents are environmentally friendly, predominantly used in applications where quick drying and ease of cleanup are essential. Solvent-based ink wetting agents, on the other hand, offer superior performance in terms of adhesion and stability in harsh conditions, making them suitable for industrial applications. Both types enhance ink flow and uniformity, improving print quality in various printing processes.</p></p>
