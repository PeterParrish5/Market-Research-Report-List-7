Straight stairlifts are designed for simple, linear staircases, featuring a straightforward installation process and lower costs. In contrast, curved stairlifts are custom-built to fit staircases with bends or turns, offering more versatility and adaptability for complex layouts but often at a higher price. Both types enhance accessibility for wheelchair users, facilitating safe and convenient movement between different levels in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
