<p><p>The polyester fibres market primarily consists of two segments: Polyester Staple Fibre (PSF) and Polyester Filament Yarn (PFY). PSF is produced from recycled PET or virgin material and is mainly used in textiles, filling materials, and non-woven applications. In contrast, PFY comprises continuous strands and is utilized for high-strength fabrics, apparel, and industrial applications. Both segments are driven by demand across various industries, including fashion, home textiles, and automotive, due to their durability, versatility, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
