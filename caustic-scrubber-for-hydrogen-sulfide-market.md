<p><p>Caustic scrubbers for hydrogen sulfide (H2S) are crucial for air pollution control, primarily categorized into two types: vertical and horizontal scrubbers. Vertical scrubbers, or counter-flow systems, allow the gas to ascend while caustic liquid descends, maximizing contact time and efficiency in removing H2S. Conversely, horizontal scrubbers, or cross-flow systems, direct gas and liquid streams perpendicularly, enhancing space efficiency and ease of maintenance. Both types effectively neutralize harmful H2S, thus serving industrial applications and environmental compliance needs.</p></p>
