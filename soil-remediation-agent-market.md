<p><p>The soil remediation agent market is primarily divided into two main types: organic and inorganic soil remediation agents. Organic soil remediation agents typically include natural compounds like plant extracts, bioenzymes, and microbial products that enhance soil health by promoting biodegradation and mineralization of contaminants. In contrast, inorganic soil remediation agents consist of chemical substances like activated carbon, clay minerals, and metal oxides, which physically or chemically bind pollutants, improving soil quality and restoring contaminated sites effectively.</p></p>
