<p><p>The VOC gas testing device market includes various technologies, primarily focusing on Photoionization Detectors (PID) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID devices utilize ultraviolet light to ionize gas molecules, providing accurate and sensitive detection of volatile organic compounds. In contrast, MOS sensors detect gases through changes in electrical resistance resulting from gas interaction with metal oxides, offering cost-effective solutions for VOC monitoring. Both technologies serve diverse applications in industrial safety, environmental monitoring, and indoor air quality assessment.</p></p>
