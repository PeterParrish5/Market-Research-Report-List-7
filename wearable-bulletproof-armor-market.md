Soft bulletproof armor is typically made from flexible materials, offering comfort and concealability while providing protection against low-caliber threats and projectiles. In contrast, hard bulletproof armor consists of rigid materials, such as steel or ceramics, designed to withstand higher caliber rounds and provide superior protection but can be bulkier and less flexible. Both types cater to different needs in personal safety and law enforcement applications.</p></p>
