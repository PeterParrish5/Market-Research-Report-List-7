<p><p>The building waterproof coating market is divided into two primary types: dry and liquid coatings. Dry coatings, such as powders or membranes, are often applied in sheets and require minimal mixing, providing durable moisture barriers for roofs and basements. Liquid coatings, on the other hand, are formulated as solutions or emulsions that can be easily applied with brushes or sprayers, offering seamless water resistance and flexibility to cover complex surfaces. Both types serve essential roles in protecting structures from water damage.</p></p>
