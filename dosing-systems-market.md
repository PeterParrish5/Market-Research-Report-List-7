<p><strong>The Dosing Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diaphragm</li><li>Piston</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dosing systems in the market primarily fall into two categories: diaphragm and piston types. Diaphragm dosing systems utilize a flexible membrane to accurately control the flow of fluids, ensuring precise dosing even under varying pressure conditions. They are commonly used in chemical processing and water treatment. Piston dosing systems operate using a reciprocating piston to dispense fluids, offering high accuracy and efficiency for thicker fluids. These systems are favored in industries like pharmaceuticals and food processing where precision is critical.</p></p>
