<p><p>The diode medical laser market encompasses various types of lasers based on their excitation methods. Electric injection lasers rely on direct electrical currents to generate laser light, ideal for precision applications. Light pump lasers use external light sources, such as flashlamps or LEDs, to excite the gain medium, offering versatility in treatment settings. High-energy electron beam excitation lasers utilize electron beams for laser generation, producing high-intensity beams suitable for advanced surgical and therapeutic procedures, enhancing clinical effectiveness.</p></p>
