<p><p>Integrated Traffic Control Systems encompass three main market types. Traffic Monitoring Systems collect real-time data on vehicle flow and congestion, enabling effective analysis and management. Traffic Control Systems utilize this data to optimize traffic signals and manage flow, enhancing road safety and efficiency. Information Provision Systems disseminate real-time updates to drivers via signs, apps, or websites, helping them make informed travel decisions. Together, these systems create a cohesive traffic management approach that improves urban mobility and reduces congestion.</p></p>
