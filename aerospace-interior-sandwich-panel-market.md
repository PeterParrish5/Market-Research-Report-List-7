<p><p>Aerospace interior sandwich panels are lightweight, high-strength composite materials used in aircraft interiors for applications such as cabin walls, ceilings, galleys, lavatories, and overhead bins. These panels typically consist of a core material, such as honeycomb or foam, sandwiched between two thin, rigid face sheets made of materials like aluminum, fiberglass, or carbon fiber. They offer advantages like weight reduction, durability, thermal insulation, and fire resistance, making them essential for modern aircraft design.</p><p>The Aerospace Interior Sandwich Panel Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.5% during the forecast period.
