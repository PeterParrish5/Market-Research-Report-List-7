<p><p>The Coronary Artery Disease Treatment Devices Market encompasses several key types. Coronary stents are mesh tubes implanted to keep arteries open post-angioplasty, reducing restenosis risk. PTCA balloon catheters are utilized to perform percutaneous transluminal coronary angioplasty, where inflatable balloons widen narrowed arteries. Coronary guidewires assist in navigating through blood vessels during these procedures, ensuring precise placement of stents and catheters. Together, these devices play a crucial role in improving blood flow and treating coronary artery disease effectively.</p></p>
