<p><p>The roll forming equipment market is categorized into two primary types based on the thickness of the material being processed. Thin coil roll forming equipment is designed for materials less than 2mm thick, ideal for producing lightweight components such as automotive parts and building profiles. In contrast, thick coil roll forming equipment accommodates materials greater than 2mm, suitable for creating heavier-duty applications like structural frames and industrial components. These distinctions are essential for meeting varying production needs across industries.</p></p>
