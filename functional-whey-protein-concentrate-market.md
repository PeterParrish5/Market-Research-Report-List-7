<p><ul><li>Whey Protein Concentrate (WPC)</li><li>Whey Protein Isolate (WPI)</li><li>Hydrolyzed Whey Protein (HWP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The functional whey protein concentrate market comprises three main types: Whey Protein Concentrate (WPC), Whey Protein Isolate (WPI), and Hydrolyzed Whey Protein (HWP). WPC contains 70-80% protein and retains beneficial nutrients, making it popular for general supplementation. WPI is highly purified, offering over 90% protein with minimal fats and carbohydrates, ideal for those seeking lean muscle growth. HWP is pre-digested for faster absorption, making it suitable for individuals with digestive issues or athletes requiring quick protein delivery.</p></p>
