<p><p>The venipuncture needle market is primarily divided into two types: soft needles and rigid needles. Soft needles are designed for greater flexibility and comfort, minimizing patient discomfort during blood draws. They are often used in sensitive populations, such as children or elderly patients. Rigid needles, on the other hand, offer higher stability and precision, making them suitable for tasks requiring greater control, such as blood donations or intravenous access. Both types cater to different clinical needs and patient preferences in healthcare settings.</p></p>
