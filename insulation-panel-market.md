VIPs offer exceptional thermal performance by minimizing heat transfer through a vacuum seal, making them ideal for space-constrained applications. SIPs combine insulation and structural components, providing both strength and thermal efficiency, commonly used in residential and commercial buildings. Other types may include conventional foam panels and reflective insulation, catering to diverse industrial and construction needs while enhancing energy efficiency.</p></p>
