<p><p>The straight seam submerged arc welded (SAW) pipe market features several manufacturing processes, notably the JCO and UOE methods. The JCO process involves shaping the steel plate into a "J" shape, then "C" and "O" for welding, ensuring strong seams. In contrast, the UOE process involves forming the plate into a "U" shape, followed by an "O" shape before welding, providing excellent dimensional precision. Other methods include spiral and longitudinal welding, catering to diverse application needs in industries like oil and gas.</p></p>
