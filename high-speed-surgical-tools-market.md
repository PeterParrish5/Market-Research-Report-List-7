In contrast, electric surgical tools operate using electrical energy, providing consistent power and speed, which is advantageous for precision tasks. Both types cater to various surgical needs, enhancing efficiency and reducing operation time, ultimately improving patient outcomes in modern surgical practices.</p></p>
