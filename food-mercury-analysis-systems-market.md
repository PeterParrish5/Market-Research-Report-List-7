<p><strong>The Food Mercury Analysis Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Type</li><li>Cold Atomic Fluorescence Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Food Mercury Analysis Systems market includes two primary types: Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption (CVAA) and Cold Atomic Fluorescence (CAF) systems. CVAA utilizes a cold vapor technique to detect mercury levels by measuring the absorption of light in a sample, providing highly sensitive results. In contrast, CAF systems rely on the fluorescence emitted by mercury atoms when exposed to specific wavelengths of light, offering rapid and efficient analysis. Both methods are crucial for ensuring food safety by monitoring mercury contamination in various food products.</p></p>
