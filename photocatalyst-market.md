<p><p>The photocatalyst market is primarily categorized into TiO2-based, ZnO-based, and other types. TiO2 (titanium dioxide) is widely used due to its efficiency and stability under UV light, making it effective for environmental applications like air and water purification. ZnO (zinc oxide) offers high photocatalytic activity and is often favored for visible light applications. Other emerging photocatalysts include materials like graphitic carbon nitride and perovskites, which are gaining traction for their unique properties and potential applicability in solar energy conversion and various chemical processes.</p></p>
