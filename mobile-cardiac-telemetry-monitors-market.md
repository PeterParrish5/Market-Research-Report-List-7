<p><ul><li>Lead Based Devices</li><li>Patch Based Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mobile Cardiac Telemetry Monitors market is primarily categorized into lead-based devices and patch-based devices. Lead-based devices utilize traditional electrodes that attach to the skin, providing accurate real-time heart monitoring, but can be cumbersome. In contrast, patch-based devices are lightweight, adhesive, and offer wireless connectivity, enhancing comfort and wearability for patients. Both types enable continuous heart activity tracking, facilitating timely intervention for arrhythmias and other cardiac issues, thus improving patient outcomes and experience in remote cardiac care.</p></p>
