<p><p>The machine stretch film market comprises several types, each with distinct properties. Pre-stretched machine stretch film is stretched during production, enhancing strength and reducing gauge, making it eco-friendly. Blown machine stretch wrap is created through a blowing process, offering excellent stretchability and puncture resistance, ideal for heavy loads. Cast machine stretch film is produced via a casting process, resulting in superior clarity and stability but lower strength than blown films. Other types may include specialized films for specific applications or industry needs.</p></p>
