<p><p>The wound care biologics market consists of three main types: biologic skin substitutes, enzyme-based formulations, and growth factors. Biologic skin substitutes mimic natural skin, promoting healing and tissue regeneration. Enzyme-based formulations enhance wound healing by breaking down dead tissue, facilitating a cleaner wound environment. Growth factors are proteins that stimulate cellular processes, accelerating tissue repair and angiogenesis. Together, these biologics provide advanced solutions to improve wound healing outcomes and are increasingly essential in modern wound management strategies.</p></p>
