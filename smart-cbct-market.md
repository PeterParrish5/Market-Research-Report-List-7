<p><ul><li>Large FOV</li><li>Medium FOV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart CBCT market is divided into two main types based on field of view (FOV): Large FOV and Medium FOV. Large FOV CBCT systems are designed to capture comprehensive 3D images of larger anatomical areas, making them ideal for complex cases in dentistry and maxillofacial imaging. In contrast, Medium FOV systems provide detailed images of smaller regions, offering higher resolution for specific applications such as endodontics and implant planning, balancing image quality with radiation exposure.</p></p>
