<p><p>The Connected Street Light Market is categorized into two main types: wired and wireless systems. Wired systems utilize Power Line Communication (PLC), where data is transmitted through existing power lines, ensuring reliable connectivity and reducing installation complexity. In contrast, wireless systems rely on technologies like Zigbee, LoRa, or cellular networks to transmit data, offering flexibility and easier deployment in remote areas. Both types enhance street lighting efficiency, improve energy management, and enable smart city integration for real-time monitoring and control.</p></p>
