<p><ul><li>AMR</li><li>AMI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Natural Gas Meter market can be divided into two key types: Automatic Meter Reading (AMR) and Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI). AMR technology enables one-way communication, allowing meters to send data to utilities for billing and monitoring without manual readings. In contrast, AMI offers two-way communication, facilitating real-time data exchange between utilities and customers, enabling dynamic pricing, demand response, and enhanced energy management. Both systems enhance efficiency, reduce operational costs, and improve customer engagement in natural gas consumption monitoring.</p></p>
