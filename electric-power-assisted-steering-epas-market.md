<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electric Power Assisted Steering (EPAS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Power Assisted Steering (EPAS) uses an electric motor to assist drivers in steering, enhancing control and fuel efficiency. The market includes three main types: **C-EPS** (Column-EPS), where the motor is mounted on the steering column, ideal for compact cars; **P-EPS** (Pinion-EPS), with the motor on the pinion shaft, suitable for mid-sized vehicles; and **R-EPS** (Rack-EPS), where the motor is directly on the steering rack, designed for larger, high-performance vehicles. Each type caters to specific vehicle needs, balancing performance, cost, and space constraints.</p></p>
