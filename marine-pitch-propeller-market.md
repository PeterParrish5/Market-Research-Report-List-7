<p><ul><li>Fixed Pitch Propeller</li><li>Controllable Pitch Propeller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine pitch propeller market primarily consists of two types: fixed pitch propellers and controllable pitch propellers. Fixed pitch propellers have blades set at a specific angle, providing efficiency for constant speed vessels, but limited maneuverability. In contrast, controllable pitch propellers allow for adjustable blade angles, enabling optimized performance across varying operating conditions, enhancing maneuverability and fuel efficiency. This versatility makes controllable pitch propellers suitable for a broader range of vessels, including tugs and specialized ships.</p></p>
