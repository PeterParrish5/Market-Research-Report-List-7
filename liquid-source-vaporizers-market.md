<p><p>Liquid source vaporizers are used to convert liquid fuel into vapor for combustion in various applications. The market is divided into two main types: direct injection and mixed injection. Direct injection vaporizers introduce the liquid directly into the combustion chamber, ensuring efficient vaporization. In contrast, mixed injection vaporizers blend liquid and gas prior to combustion, enhancing combustion efficiency and reducing emissions. Both types cater to specific industrial needs, optimizing performance based on combustion requirements and fuel characteristics.</p></p>
