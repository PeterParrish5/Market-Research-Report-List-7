<p><p>The Hem Flange Bonding Adhesive market is divided into two primary types: one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are ready-to-use formulations that cure upon exposure to air or heat, offering convenience and quick application. In contrast, two-component adhesives consist of a resin and a hardener that must be mixed before use, providing enhanced bonding strength and versatility for various applications. Both types cater to different requirements in industries like automotive and construction, ensuring effective hem flange bonding solutions.</p></p>
