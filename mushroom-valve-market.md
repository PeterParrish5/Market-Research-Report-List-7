<p><p>Mushroom valves are classified into three main types based on their configuration and functionality. </p><p>- **2-way valves** allow fluid flow in one direction or the other, providing basic on/off control. </p><p>- **3-way valves** can direct flow between two outlets, enabling mixing or diversion of fluids. </p><p>- **4-way valves** manage the flow in four different directions, often used in complex applications like pneumatic systems to control actuators. Each type serves specific operational needs in various industrial processes.</p></p>
