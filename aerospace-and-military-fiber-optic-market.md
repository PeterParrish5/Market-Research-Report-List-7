<p><p>The Aerospace and Military Fiber Optic market includes two primary types: single-mode and multi-mode fiber optics. Single-mode fibers utilize a single light path, allowing for longer transmission distances and higher bandwidth, ideal for aerospace applications requiring minimal signal loss. Multi-mode fibers, on the other hand, support multiple light paths, enabling shorter distance communication with higher data rates over limited ranges, making them suitable for military applications where quick, robust data transfer is essential in confined environments.</p></p>
