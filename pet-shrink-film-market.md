<p><p>The PET shrink film market is categorized into low, medium, and high shrink films based on their shrinkage properties. Low shrink films typically exhibit minimal shrinkage, making them suitable for light-duty applications like labels. Medium shrink films offer moderate shrinkage, ideal for standard packaging needs. High shrink films, which provide maximum shrinkage, are used in demanding applications such as securing heavy items or odd shapes. Each type serves different packaging requirements, ensuring efficiency and effectiveness in product presentation and protection.</p></p>
