The market is divided into two main types: forged steel grinding balls and cast steel grinding balls. Forged steel grinding balls are produced through a forging process, resulting in higher strength and uniformity, making them ideal for high-impact applications. In contrast, cast steel grinding balls are created by casting molten metal, offering a cost-effective solution suitable for many industries but typically with lower hardness compared to forged options.</p></p>
