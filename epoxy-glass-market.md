<p><p>The epoxy glass market encompasses various manufacturing processes: </p><p>1. **Lay-up** involves manual placement of epoxy and glass layers for custom parts.</p><p>2. **Compression molding** uses heat and pressure to shape pre-formed materials into components.</p><p>3. **Resin injection** injects epoxy resin into a mold, enhancing design complexity and detail. </p><p>4. **Resin transfer molding** transfers resin into closed molds for high-strength applications. </p><p>5. **Filament winding** wraps epoxy-impregnated fibers around a mandrel for cylindrical parts. </p><p>6. **Pultrusion** continuously pulls materials through resin and a heated die to create consistent profiles.</p></p>
