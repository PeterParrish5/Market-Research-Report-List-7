<p><strong>The Automotive Solar Control Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbing Solar Control Glass</li><li>Reflective Solar Control Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive solar control glass market consists of two primary types: absorbing solar control glass and reflective solar control glass. Absorbing solar control glass is designed to absorb and reduce solar heat gain, enhancing interior comfort and minimizing air conditioning needs. In contrast, reflective solar control glass features a coated surface that reflects sunlight away, significantly reducing glare and heat entry. Both types aim to improve vehicle energy efficiency, passenger comfort, and overall driving experience while offering protection from harmful UV rays.</p></p>
