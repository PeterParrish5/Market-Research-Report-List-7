<p><p>An automotive grommet is a small, often rubber or plastic component used in vehicles to protect and secure wires, cables, and hoses passing through holes in metal or plastic panels. Grommets are widely used in electrical systems, engine compartments, and interior components, making them essential for vehicle functionality and longevity.</p><p>The Automotive Grommet Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4% during the forecast period. Overall, the automotive grommet market is poised for steady growth, driven by technological advancements and evolving automotive needs.</p></p>
