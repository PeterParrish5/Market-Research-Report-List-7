<p><p>Cryo vacuum pumps are categorized into horizontal and vertical types based on their orientation and design. Horizontal cryo vacuum pumps are positioned with the intake and exhaust ports aligned horizontally, allowing for easier integration in constrained spaces and facilitating maintenance. In contrast, vertical cryo vacuum pumps are designed with a vertical orientation, optimizing space utilization and enhancing gravity-assisted operation. Both types are essential in applications requiring ultra-high vacuum levels, such as semiconductor manufacturing and research facilities.</p></p>
