<p><p>The Industrial Electrodialysis System market features two primary types: Continuous Electrodialysis and Batch Electrodialysis. Continuous Electrodialysis involves a steady flow of feed solution through the system, allowing for constant operation and efficient separation of ions, making it ideal for large-scale processes. In contrast, Batch Electrodialysis entails processing a fixed volume of solution at a time, offering flexibility and control over the treatment process, suitable for smaller, more specialized applications. Both methods are vital for ion separation and purification.</p></p>
