<p><p>The flat-field scanning lens market encompasses various types designed for specific applications. F-theta scanning lenses produce a flat focal plane, ensuring consistent focus across the field of view, making them ideal for high-speed laser marking and imaging applications. In contrast, telecentric scanning lenses maintain a constant magnification and eliminate parallax errors, making them suitable for precision measurement and inspection tasks. Both lens types enhance image quality and accuracy in diverse industrial and scientific settings.</p></p>
