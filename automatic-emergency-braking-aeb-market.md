<p><ul><li>Forward Collision Warning</li><li>Dynamic Brake Support</li><li>Crash Imminent Braking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic Emergency Braking (AEB) is a safety system that helps prevent collisions by automatically applying brakes when a potential crash is detected. The market includes three key types: **Forward Collision Warning (FCW)**, which alerts drivers of impending collisions; **Dynamic Brake Support (DBS)**, which amplifies braking force if the driver's response is insufficient; and **Crash Imminent Braking (CIB)**, which autonomously applies full braking to avoid or mitigate a crash. These systems enhance vehicle safety by reducing accidents and minimizing impact severity.</p></p>
