<p><p>Dynamic Voltage Restorers (DVR) are power electronic devices designed to maintain voltage levels in electrical systems by compensating for voltage sags and swells. The market for DVRs is categorized into two types: single-phase and three-phase output. Single-phase DVRs serve residential or small commercial applications, providing voltage support to individual loads. In contrast, three-phase DVRs cater to industrial and large commercial setups, ensuring stable voltage across multiple phases, thus enhancing power quality and system reliability in more complex electrical networks.</p></p>
