<p><ul><li>Vertical Axis</li><li>Horizontal Axis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Trailed Aerator Market is primarily categorized into two types based on their axis orientation: Vertical Axis and Horizontal Axis. Vertical Axis Trailed Aerators use a vertical rotating mechanism to effectively penetrate and aerate the soil, promoting enhanced soil health and plant growth. In contrast, Horizontal Axis Trailed Aerators utilize a horizontal rotation to achieve similar results, targeting aeration across wider swathes. Both designs are crucial for agricultural efficiency, optimizing soil conditions for various crops while improving water absorption and nutrient distribution.</p></p>
