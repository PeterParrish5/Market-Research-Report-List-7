<p><p>The elastomeric thermal rubber market comprises several types, including NBR-based, EPDM-based, and chloroprene-based rubbers, along with other formulations. NBR (nitrile butadiene rubber) is known for its oil resistance, making it ideal for automotive applications. EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) excels in weather resistance and is often utilized for roofing and automotive seals. Chloroprene rubber offers excellent aging and weathering properties, commonly used in coatings and adhesives. Other types may include silicone or polyurethane, broadening application scopes across various industries.</p></p>
