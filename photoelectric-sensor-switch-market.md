<p><p>The photoelectric sensor switch market is categorized into two primary types: laser and fiber optics. Laser photoelectric sensors utilize focused light beams for precise detection, offering high accuracy and long-range capabilities, making them suitable for challenging applications. Fiber optic sensors, on the other hand, leverage flexible fiber strands to transmit light, allowing for versatile installations in confined or hazardous environments. Both types enhance automation and safety in industries such as manufacturing, logistics, and automotive, driving advancements in operational efficiency.</p></p>
