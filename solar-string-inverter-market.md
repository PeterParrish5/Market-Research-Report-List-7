<p><p>A solar string inverter is a type of inverter used in photovoltaic systems to convert direct current (DC) generated by solar panels into alternating current (AC) suitable for use in homes and businesses. Unlike central inverters that manage power from multiple panels, string inverters handle power from a series of connected solar panels, or "strings."
