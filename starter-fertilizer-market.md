<p><strong>The Starter Fertilizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ortho-phosphate</li><li>Poly-phosphate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The starter fertilizer market primarily features two types of phosphorus sources: ortho-phosphate and poly-phosphate. Ortho-phosphate is immediately available for plant uptake, making it effective for promoting early root development. In contrast, poly-phosphate offers a slower release of nutrients, enhancing soil stability and providing a longer-lasting supply of phosphorus. Both types cater to different agricultural needs, with ortho-phosphate being ideal for quick absorption during critical growth phases, while poly-phosphate supports sustained nutrient availability over time.</p></p>
