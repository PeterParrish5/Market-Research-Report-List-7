<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Private Mortgage Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Borrower-paid (BPMI)</li><li>Lender-paid (LPMI)</li><li>Single Premium</li><li>Split Premium</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Private Mortgage Insurance (PMI) protects lenders from borrower default. Borrower-paid PMI (BPMI) involves monthly premiums added to the mortgage payment, allowing homeowners to cancel it once they reach 20% equity. Lender-paid PMI (LPMI) is absorbed by the lender, resulting in higher interest rates but no separate PMI payment. Single premium PMI involves an upfront lump-sum payment at closing, while split premium PMI combines an upfront payment with reduced monthly premiums, offering flexibility based on borrower preferences and financial situations.</p></p>
