<p><p>The electric truck charging pile market is primarily categorized into DC (Direct Current) and AC (Alternating Current) charging piles. DC charging piles provide rapid charging by delivering high power directly to the vehicle's battery, enabling quick turnaround times for fleet operations. In contrast, AC charging piles offer a more gradual charging process, typically used for overnight charging or in locations where high power access is limited. Both types are essential for supporting the growing electric truck infrastructure and meeting diverse charging needs.</p></p>
