<p><strong>The HLA Typing Transplant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Molecular Assays</li><li>Molecular Assays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HLA typing for transplantation involves determining the human leukocyte antigen profile to ensure compatibility between donors and recipients. The market is divided into two main types: non-molecular assays and molecular assays. Non-molecular assays, such as serological tests, rely on antibody-antigen interactions but may lack precision. In contrast, molecular assays utilize advanced techniques like PCR and sequencing to provide more accurate and detailed insights into HLA variations, leading to better transplant outcomes and reduced rejection rates.</p></p>
