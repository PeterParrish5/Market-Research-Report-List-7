<p><p>The epoxy resin industrial coating market is divided into three main types: Solvent Type, Waterborne Type, and Non-Solvent Type. Solvent Type coatings use organic solvents for application, providing excellent performance but raising environmental concerns. Waterborne Type coatings are eco-friendlier, utilizing water as a primary solvent, leading to lower volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Non-Solvent Type coatings, such as 100% solids formulations, contain no solvents, offering high coating thickness and reduced emissions, making them increasingly popular in various industrial applications.</p></p>
