<p><ul><li>Wet Tile Cutting Machine</li><li>Dry Tile Cutting Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tile cutting machine market is primarily divided into wet and dry tile cutting machines. Wet tile cutting machines use water to cool the blade and reduce dust, making them ideal for cutting hard materials like porcelain and granite. This method provides smoother cuts and minimizes inhalation hazards. Conversely, dry tile cutting machines operate without water, making them more portable and convenient for quick jobs, but they generate more dust and can be suited for softer materials. Both types serve essential roles in construction and renovation projects.</p></p>
