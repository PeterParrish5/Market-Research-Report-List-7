<p><p>Interventional fluoroscopy systems are classified into two main types: those with a C-arm and those with a table. C-arm systems are portable and allow flexibility in positioning, ideal for various procedures, including orthopedic and vascular interventions. In contrast, systems with a table provide a stable platform, facilitating more complex surgeries and real-time imaging during procedures. Both types enhance visualization and precision, improving patient outcomes in minimally invasive surgeries and diagnostic interventions.</p></p>
