<p><p>The Ballistic Deflection Transistor (BDT) market primarily consists of two types: NPN and PNP transistors. NPN transistors have a structure that allows electrons to flow from the emitter to the collector, making them suitable for high-speed applications. In contrast, PNP transistors enable holes to move from the emitter to the collector, providing advantages in specific circuit designs. Both types play crucial roles in modern electronics, each catering to distinct operational requirements and contribute significantly to advancements in semiconductor technology.</p></p>
